Overview
This project creates an infographic highlighting the rise of Kia and Hyundai thefts, particularly in Milwaukee. It was designed for everyday citizens to understand the scope and urgency of the issue.
Dataset
•	Vehicle theft reports provided for analysis
•	Summarized and cleaned for visualization clarity
Methods
•	Exploratory data analysis
•	Data visualization with simple charts and bold callouts
•	Infographic design for easy readability
Results
The infographic showed Kia and Hyundai thefts spiking in cities, with Milwaukee being heavily affected. The call-to-action encouraged people to secure their vehicles and demand accountability.
Tools
Python (Matplotlib), Canva/infographic design tools
Future Work
•	Expand to include national data comparisons
•	Track theft trends over time
•	Suggest predictive models for high-risk areas
